Old School Runescape or Runescape 3?

Comparing Old School Runescape and Runescape 3

Old school Runescape

  • Basic combat system
  • Slower paced gameplay
  • Minigames
  • Social activities
  • Player vs Player combat
  • No Microtransactions, except bonds
  • Is a solid game and the developers would never dare ruin because it has such a hardcore playerbase that would be gone in the blink of an eye
